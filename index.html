Project Idea

Overview

My project idea is aimed at artists who like to draw but be able to use that ability on a computer without the use of a mouse The skills to replicate the small micro adjustments as if it was like drawing on a piece of paper and transmitting that to the computer with a program. The use of a touch sensitive pad and a pen will create this feeling. There will be a sequence of items used and data needed.

Motivation

This project will be useful in many ways to help artists explore options than stuck drawing on a piece of paper. It’s to use IT in a way to help branch artists to use what they’ve learned with the basic and expand their understanding of using different effects and save resources. This will save them more on specific color pallets or pencils since all they would need to do is go to a color sheet and select the exact type of color, they want without paying a single dollar, and it’s not limited use. This develops a artist to put more time into their pieces and if they make a mistake on a piece of paper, it will definitely show since you can’t just undo what you just drew.

Description

This product will use a touch sensitive pad to transmit what would be drawn down but instead, it will run through a code and give the user the ability to draw on a pc without the use of a mouse and instead a pen. This gives artist the counter to going through stuff like pens and ink when wanting to create art as well as the use to undo and create layers so that their work is separate and can put more time in their work than to utilize the resources. With this project idea it will require a program that can transmute micro adjustments and make it super sensitive, so the user has a precise feel for what they are creating, as if it were a pen on paper. Most talk about towards artist could be the price of resources available to them, some might use spray cans, and some might use Copic markers. They can get pretty cost affective since they are ink and once placed on paper can change a drawing either for the good or for the bad. What if I was to tell you that there could be no bad? Factoring the cost for markers or resources and the mistakes, this project can eliminate those issues for artists. This also could come at the cost of a 10 pack of markers for an unlimited color pallet and resources depending on how much storage you have. Endless area for creation and to make sure artists have the freedom and ability to create anything is the purpose of this project. The aim of this is to help artist feel like they aren’t restricted and give them the ability to explore new options. The pad will be calculated to its borders, The program will let you edit this to fit and specifications the user wants. It will automatically be synced to the resolution of your device.

Tools and Technologies

The tools needed is 3d imaging and programming. These skills are the biggest thing involved since it all depends on this build quality of the item and the quality of the code. There will be lots of testing involved to make sure there are no errors 100% but that’s the beauty of this, its to work on not to be completed 100% fully. These will need to be durable and not fragile. The work on the structure and how sleek you can make it depends on the imagination and how much access material you can get rid of, which all factors on how much space you have.

Outcome

This will give artists the ability to convert from paper and pens to digital. This increases their skill and chance to try new things they can’t without technology.
